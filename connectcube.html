Product
Overview

Connect Cube explores implicit communication with our families even while living far apart.

Design Process

Discovery Research

I conducted surveys and interviews to better understand communication behaviors between potential users and their relatives, and to acquire information about the personal need to feel connected and ways to enhance that connection.

Define

The surveys and interviews highlight behaviors and motivations. I focus on a specific demographic group: women below 45, who usually communicate with parents or close relatives on a daily basis or at least check up to assure that everything is okay. A Persona, Empathy Map and Experience Map was developed from the discovery phase.

User Interviews - Findings

The research is oriented around how people feel about the quality of communication, what other options they might consider and whether they envision other effective communication with loved ones.

Based on the survey and interviews I gained Insights:

‚óè Constant communication relieves anxiety about their well-being.

‚óè People would like to be in constant communication with their relatives.

‚óè Most of them feel that the distance affects the relationship.

‚óè Users mostly communicate through phone calls and text. However, they haven,t find an option less disruptive to share time with them.

Develop

Ideate

Inspired by our current socially distant lives, I searched for a unique interaction to strengthen our connections. A series of sketches helped me visualize the concept and possible interaction models. This process resulted in a set of cubes that, when a person joins them together, sends a message to the other person.

Storyboard

The story takes place in their homes. They make a connection using the cubes. The storyboard illustrates the casual use of the cubes and how easy it is to share a moment.

User Interaction

The person is invited to join objects together to share their activities with another person. This message consists of an activity they want the other person to know about, an emotion or a feeling. The activity cubes are interchangable.

User Testing & Iterations

I conducted 2 user testing sessions: for the physical interaction and for the app onboarding process. Based on these findings, I was able to iterate design and make improvements on the interactions.

Remote Usability Test

To simulate the interaction, I rapidly coded a custom remote user test.
These tests allowed me to check my assumptions and quickly gain insight into problems in the interaction and the intuitiveness.

Usability Test - App

Also I test the process of activating the product.
