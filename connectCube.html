Connect Cube

UX/UI IoT Development

For families who live far apart from each other, it’s a constant challenge to maintain an meaningful connection. They must actively make the effort to stay in contact. For our closest relationships, a connection goes beyond active communication. If there was a better way to share moments from a distance, then families could feel like they are together. This project its aimed to explore implicit communications with our families while living far apart

  • Process:
    User Research (surveys, Interviews), Persona, Experience Map, Storyboard, User Flow, Mood board, Wireframes, User Testing, Digital Fabrication, Prototyping
  • Tools:
    Figma, Fusion 360, Javascript, MongoDB, Arduino, Ps, Ai, Google Forms

Define and Discovery

Discovery research

I worked on a survey and interviews to better understand what are the communication behaviors of the potential users with their relatives, gain more knowledge about the feeling to be connected and which ways they feel that they could enhance that connection.

Define

The surveys and the interview let me highlight the possible, their behaviors and motivations. I concentrated on the people that most fill the survey, woman below 45, mostly they communicate with their parents and close relatives on a daily basis to make sure that they are ok. Persona, Empathy Map and Experience Map was developed from the discovery phase.

User Interviews - Findings

The research were oriented about how she feels about the quality of communication, what other options she could consider and what other ways she can envision her communication.

Design process

Ideate

Inspired by our current event that we live with with concious social distancing, this project is intended to create a unique interaction that strengthens our connections. I worked on a series of sketches for this concept. My approach was to create a set of cubes that, when a person joins them together, can send a message to another person.

Storyboard

The story takes place in their homes. They make a connection with the blocks. In each frame, I want to communicate the casualness of using the device and sharing the moment.

UX Design

I developed how would flow the main interaction. The interaction is personal. The person is invited to join the objects to share their activities with another person that has the same system of cubes. This message consists of activities that they want the other person to know, an emotion or perhaps a feeling. I developed a Moodboard, User Flow and Wireframes.

User Flow

The idea behind the activities is to share a moment with the other user. User 1 is the first to initiate the interaction, and the User 2 respond to the share connection.

App User Flow

Activation Process
