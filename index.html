Sounds of the Amazon

Data Visualization | Visual Journalism | Visual Design

My Role: Data Visualization

Tools: P5.js, Ai, D3, Flourish, Observable

Team: Themis Garcia, Vanessa Chesnut and Sarah Liriano

This project explores the Biophony of the Amazon by analyzing acoustic data. By leveraging sound, we are able to identify animals that live within an ecosystem and establish a frequency range for each animal. This information helps visualize biodiversity and develop a story for a habitat.

Hurricane Data Visualization

Visual Design | Data Visualization

Tools: p5.js, Figma

A series of visualizations of NOAA's historic hurricane data set between 2016 and 2017. The exploration includes an app that visualizes the direction of the storm, distance from land, landfall, wind speed, and storm category.
The list of hurricanes is navigable via the interface.

Using Observable and D3, wind speed and landfall are visualized on a graph.

Food Sovereignty in Puerto Rico

Tech + Activism | UX Research | UX Design

Tools: Figma

Is it possible to design a system that encourages solidarity with local and rural economies?

Through research, I gained insights from the community and discovered a realm of opportunities. I present a platform where consumers and farmers can have a direct relationship with each other, improving visibility and shortening the supply-chain for locally grown crops.
