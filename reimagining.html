Design Process

Design Research Overview

We used several research methodologies such as ecosystem mapping, proto-personas and interviews to better understand the problem space and the users. Focusing on prototyping a set of guidelines for high school teachers and evaluated them through generative research.

Define

In this phase was developed an ecosystem map to evaluate the resources to gather information. For primary research, we focused on teachers and students.

Problem Space

Due to the circumstances defined by the Covid-19 pandemic in the United States, school districts have embraced, at different levels, the remote learning experience as a method to continue the students education process.
High School students that are in the process of preparing for adulthood and/or higher education, are aware of the constant challenges that this represents for their development.
For teachers, it is a constant challenge to measure the learning expectations of their students.
Because of different factors such as mental well-being, home support, access to internet and bandwidth, access to resources, learning accommodations, these challenges are greater for some students than others. .

Research Questions

How does the remote learning experience affect high school students’ performance?

Takeaways

Teachers are willing to embrace whatever nees to be done to help ther students success.

However, Students difficulties, union and department education agreements are different in different districs and states. However, there is a Lack of preparation for online education: Many schools and districts prepared over the summer thinking they were going to have in-person classes, but the rise in Covid cases changed the ability to implement those plans. In the US, each district is embracing different strategies, tools andapproaches:Each teacher we talked to uses a different set of tools or apps.Not all teachers record their class sessionsSome teachers have to deliver materials by mail to students who live in remotelocations

Synthesis

Remote learning has represented a challenge for the education community at different levels. Teachers are on the frontline of the development of this experience. Because of the stress due to sudden change in distance education, teachers and students have experienced difficulties. This research was oriented at understanding the distance learning environment and how to improve it in a landscape so diverse with different reglamentation, and diverse social-economical, access and educational realities.

🌻Foward Thinking: Acknowledging that distance learning should be embraced as an educational practice that is is going to stay. This guideline is a step to address some of the challenges that remote learning represent.
